Total Torque Job Card App - Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Total Torque ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information of users ("you") of the Total Torque Job Card App (the "App").

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Job Card Data: Information you enter into job cards, such as company names, registration numbers, dates, mileages, defects, work done, spares used, expenses, images, and signatures.
  • User Information (if applicable): If you sign in with an account, we may collect your email address and name.
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage data to improve the App, such as feature usage and crash reports.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To generate and manage job cards.
  • To provide you with access to the App's features.
  • To improve the App and its functionality.
  • To communicate with you about updates and support (if applicable).

4. Data Storage and Security

We store your information using the following methods:

  • Local Storage: Job card data is stored locally on your device.
  • Cloud Storage (if applicable): If you enable cloud storage, your job card data will be stored securely on Firebase Cloud Storage.

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, but we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Sharing

We may share your information with the following parties:

  • Service Providers: We may use third-party service providers (e.g., Firebase) to store and process your information.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law or legal process.

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights:

  • To access your information.
  • To correct inaccuracies in your information.
  • To request deletion of your information (subject to legal requirements).

7. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post any changes on this page and update the "Last Updated" date below.
